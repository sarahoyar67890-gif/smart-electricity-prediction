EXPLORATORY DATA ANALYSIS - BUSINESS INSIGHTS
=======================================================

Dataset size after cleaning: 1200 records, 23 columns

Average daily consumption: 17.35 kWh
Median daily consumption: 13.96 kWh
Std deviation: 9.44 kWh

Top factors INCREASING electricity consumption:
  - AC_Usage_Hours: correlation = 0.786
  - AC_Units: correlation = 0.544
  - Outdoor_Temperature_C: correlation = 0.393

Factors most associated with LOWER consumption:
  - Season_enc: correlation = -0.096
  - Lighting_Hours: correlation = -0.143
  - Refrigerator_Usage_Hours: correlation = nan

Highest consuming season: Summer (avg 23.00 kWh/day)
Lowest consuming season: Spring (avg 12.41 kWh/day)

Highest consuming house type: Bungalow (avg 24.11 kWh/day)

Business Implication: AC usage and outdoor temperature are the
dominant drivers of consumption, suggesting energy-saving campaigns
should focus on cooling appliance efficiency during summer/monsoon months.
